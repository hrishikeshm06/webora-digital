Industry Marketing
Frameworks

Each framework below demonstrates the strategic approach we apply to a given industry type. These are educational examples of how we think — not representations of actual client results.

⚠ Sample Strategy Demonstration
Real Estate
Real Estate Lead Generation Framework
🏠

A strategic framework for generating qualified property enquiries for real estate developers and agents using a multi-channel paid approach.

  • 01 Audience mapping — identify buyer personas by income level, location preference, and intent signals
  • 02 Meta Ads awareness campaign with property highlight creative targeting defined geographies
  • 03 Lead capture via landing page with virtual tour opt-in and instant WhatsApp confirmation
  • 04 Google Search campaigns targeting high-intent keywords (e.g. "2BHK flats in [area]")
  • 05 Automated follow-up sequence via WhatsApp to qualify and book site visits
  • 06 Retargeting campaign for website visitors who didn't convert
⚠ Sample Strategy Demonstration
Fitness
Gym Membership Funnel Framework
🏋️

A lead-to-member conversion framework for gyms, fitness studios, and health clubs looking to grow their membership base with paid social.

  • 01 Hyper-local Meta Ads targeting within 5–8 km radius using interest and behavioural audiences
  • 02 Offer-led creative — free trial, first-week pass, or transformation challenge as lead magnet
  • 03 Landing page with social proof, facility tour, and low-friction booking form
  • 04 Instant WhatsApp message to every new lead for personal welcome and visit scheduling
  • 05 7-day follow-up sequence for leads who haven't visited yet
  • 06 Member referral campaign once initial members are enrolled
⚠ Sample Strategy Demonstration
Finance & Insurance
Insurance Lead Generation Framework
🛡️

A trust-first lead generation framework for insurance advisors, mutual fund distributors, and financial planners looking to generate consultation bookings.

  • 01 Educational content campaign — awareness ads built around common financial mistakes and solutions
  • 02 Google Search campaigns targeting specific insurance/investment queries with buying intent
  • 03 Lead magnet — free policy review or financial health checklist as consultation trigger
  • 04 Landing page optimised for trust signals, advisor credentials, and easy booking
  • 05 Calendly or WhatsApp-based consultation booking flow
  • 06 Retargeting campaign for content viewers who haven't booked yet
⚠ Sample Strategy Demonstration
Local Business
Local Business Growth Framework
🏪

A complete growth framework for local service businesses — salons, restaurants, clinics, coaching centres — looking to dominate their local area.

  • 01 Google Business Profile optimisation for maximum local search visibility
  • 02 Hyper-local Meta Ads campaign with neighbourhood-specific targeting and offer
  • 03 Conversion-optimised landing page or booking page with live availability
  • 04 WhatsApp broadcast system for offer announcements to existing customers
  • 05 Monthly content calendar for Instagram to maintain local visibility
  • 06 Review generation system to build trust through social proof
⚠ Sample Strategy Demonstration
Education
Education Lead Generation Framework
🎓

A lead generation framework for coaching institutes, online course creators, and educational institutions looking to fill their programme seats consistently.

  • 01 Outcome-led Meta Ads targeting parents and students by age group, location, and interest
  • 02 Free webinar or demo class as a lead magnet to build trust before enrolment
  • 03 Landing page focused on outcomes, faculty credentials, and student success stories
  • 04 WhatsApp-based enquiry flow with instant response and brochure delivery
  • 05 Google Search campaign targeting high-intent admission keywords
  • 06 Email and WhatsApp nurture sequence for leads who haven't enrolled yet
